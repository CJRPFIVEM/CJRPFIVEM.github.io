JCRP

Freedom Roleplay

JCRP is a FiveM Freeroam/Roleplay Server which gives players the freedom to do what they want.

Many servers we went to simply lacked the freedom aspect we were looking for so we decided to make our own server and share it with you!

To Play JCRP head to FiveM and search for JCRP and select our server from the list!

Our Server includes;

  • Police Job For Anyone!(FivePD)
  • Addon Map Locations
  • 100+ Addon Vehicles
  • Custom Weapons
  • Custom Skins
  • Fun Scripts
  • Jobs
